Why calisthenics?

Calisthenics is based on natural movement as it trains the body to pull, push and hold in a way it was designed to.

It not only helps to build muscle, burn fat, increase strength, but also to perform better in everyday life.

Since you can do calisthenics with minimal equipment, it's the perfect way to build the physique you want at home.

Who are we?

We are a small team of calisthenics enthusiasts, medical doctors and athletes who build graviflex.com to spread the amazing benefits of bodyweight training.

Our number one goal is to create and curate the highest quality resources to help anyone save time and achieve their fitness goals more effectively.

We care about the balance between fast progress and safe exercising — the only way to maintain a sustainable long-term routine.
